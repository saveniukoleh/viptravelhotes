About us

Vip travel Hotels was established in Istanbul to provide professional solutions to its customer’s personal and corporate travel needs.

Our agency makes Hotel Reservation, Customized Travel Programs in all around turkey and also Daily Istanbul Tours for our valued customers.

If you are planning to explore Turkey, we can prepare you personalized travel plans and accommodation arrangements "tailor made" to your demands. Our holiday destinations include Cappadocia, Pamukkale Travertine, Antalya, Bodrum, İstanbul, Trabzon as well as a number of popular and attractive holiday destinations all around the country.
